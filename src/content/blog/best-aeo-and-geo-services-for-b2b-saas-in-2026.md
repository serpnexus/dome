B2B software buyers no longer search. They ask. Some services build AI visibility through independent, off-site signals: community discussions, editorial placements, brand mentions across platforms AI models actively cite. This guide compares five of the most prominent AEO and GEO services available to B2B SaaS companies in 2026, with clear notes on what each does well and where each falls short.

## How AI Models Decide What to Recommend

Traditional SEO is a ranking problem: optimize signals Google's algorithm weights and your page moves up. AEO is a trust problem: give AI models enough independent, cross-source evidence that your brand is a known, credible entity in your category — and they will cite you.

The mechanism is query fan-out. When a user asks a broad question, the model decomposes it and retrieves from multiple source types simultaneously: Reddit threads, editorial reviews, listicle placements, review platforms, community forums. A brand that appears consistently across those independent sources accumulates what researchers call **proof density** — the threshold of cross-source evidence that causes AI models to treat a brand as citable.

The implication: owned content optimization alone — no matter how well-structured — covers only a fraction of the sources AI models pull from. Off-site, independent signals are not optional for consistent AI visibility. They are the mechanism.

## 5. Traditional SEO Agencies — A Note on Limitations

BrightEdge, Conductor, and the mid-market SEO agencies below them have all updated service descriptions to reference AI search optimization. In practice, most of what is offered under that label is on-page optimization and technical SEO work with AI-readability improvements layered on top of existing practice.

The structural issue is not execution quality — many of these agencies execute technical SEO well. The issue is that the ranking signals they optimize for (domain authority, backlink profiles, keyword density) are different from the trust signals AI models weight. Independent editorial mentions, authentic community discussions, and cross-platform brand presence feed AI citation in ways that backlink acquisition and page speed optimization do not.

Well-structured owned content and strong technical foundations are useful inputs to AEO — they improve AI crawler accessibility and content extractability. But they cover only the properties a company controls, not the independent third-party sources AI models synthesize most heavily. Used as a complement to dedicated AEO execution rather than a replacement, traditional SEO delivers real supporting value.

**Best for:** Established brands optimizing existing content infrastructure as a foundation layer alongside dedicated AEO execution.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is proof density?

Proof density is the volume and diversity of independent, third-party sources that reference a brand across the web. AI models treat cross-source mentions as a trust signal — a brand referenced in Reddit discussions, editorial articles, review platforms, and community forums is treated as more credible than one appearing only on its own site. Building proof density is the core mechanism of effective AEO.

### How long before results appear in AI search?

Omnichannel approaches building Reddit, guest posts, and brand mentions simultaneously typically produce measurable AI citation results within 60 to 90 days, because these third-party sources are indexed and referenced in near-real-time. Content-only approaches that depend on training data cycles take longer — often 6 to 12 months before new content influences AI model outputs at scale.

### Is Reddit really important for B2B SaaS AEO?

Yes. Reddit is consistently among the most cited sources in ChatGPT and Perplexity responses for B2B software comparison queries. Authentic participation in relevant subreddits — genuine contributions with real karma — carries the kind of independent community credibility AI models weight heavily. Promotional posting gets removed. Authentic community engagement compounds over time.

### How does AEO interact with my existing SEO investment?

AEO and SEO are complementary and should run simultaneously. Strong domain authority, high-quality content, and clean technical infrastructure improve both Google rankings and AI crawler accessibility. The gap is that SEO programs address only content your company controls. AEO requires off-site, independent signals — community presence, editorial placements, third-party mentions — that most SEO programs do not include. The right structure is SEO for Google alongside dedicated AEO for AI platforms.
